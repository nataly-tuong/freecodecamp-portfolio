This lasagna is from allrecipes, it is described as one of the best lasgna recipes ever and is extremely popular.

Ingredients

  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
